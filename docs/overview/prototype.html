How It Works

Structured sourcing flow built for serious automotive operations.

The prototype demonstrates a clear enterprise workflow: request submission, supplier response, option comparison, platform payment, and coordinated delivery with milestone tracking.

Step 01

Submit vehicle and part request

Customer enters make, model, VIN, part details, urgency, destination country, and supporting images.

Step 02

Suppliers respond with offers

Approved suppliers from Africa and Saudi Arabia receive RFQs and return verified quotations.

Step 03

Compare OEM, aftermarket, and used

Customer reviews price, warranty, rating, stock level, and ETA in one consistent decision view.

Step 04

Pay through the platform

Procurement summary, invoice state, payment confirmation, and escrow-ready flow are presented transparently.

Step 05

Coordinated delivery and tracking

Africa Autopart manages dispatch, customs, courier updates, and delivery confirmation in one place.
