How Africa Autopart Works

A five-step enterprise sourcing flow from part request to door delivery — transparent, trackable, and trusted.

1
📋
Submit Request
Customer submits vehicle details, part name, condition preference, and urgency. Supports image uploads and VIN lookup.
2
🔔
Suppliers Respond
Verified suppliers across Africa and KSA receive the RFQ and submit competitive offers within hours.
3
⚖️
Compare & Select
Customer reviews OEM, aftermarket, and used options — comparing price, warranty, delivery time, and supplier rating.
4
💳
Secure Payment
Funds held in platform escrow. Supplier is paid upon confirmed delivery. Supports bank transfer, mobile money, and card.
5
📦
Live Tracking
Africa Autopart coordinates logistics, customs, and last-mile delivery with real-time updates at every stage.
